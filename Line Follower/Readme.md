## Line Follower

**Line Follower Robot** is a simple autonomously guided robot that follows a line drawn on the ground to either detect a dark line on a white surface or a white line on a dark.

**Components:**
 - Arduino Uno	
 - Breadboard
 - Two IR Sensors
 - Two Gear Motors
 - H-bridge Motor Driver (L293D)
 - 9V Battery
 - Wires
